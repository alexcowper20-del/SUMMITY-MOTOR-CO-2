Our Story

About Summit

Who We Are

Passionate About Every Vehicle

Summit Motor Company was established to bring a refreshingly personal approach to the used car market. We believe that every vehicle has a story, and every buyer deserves the time and attention to find the right one.

From our premises in Holmfirth, West Yorkshire, we handpick a carefully curated selection of pre-owned vehicles — from practical family cars and commercial vehicles to performance models.

Operating by appointment only, every customer receives our full, undivided attention. No rushing, no pressure — just honest, expert guidance.
